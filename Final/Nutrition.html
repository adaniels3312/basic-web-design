WHY FRUIT MATTERS

Fruits are packed with essential nutrients, fiber, antioxidants, and natural sugars. They support overall health, help prevent chronic disease, and are deliciously hydrating due to high water content. fruits are naturally low in fat and calories, making them a great option for maintaining a healthy weight. Their natural sugars provide a quick energy boost without the crash associated with processed snacks. Moreover, fruits add flavor, color, and texture to meals, making healthy eating more enjoyable and satisfying. By including more fruit in daily meals, individuals can enhance their overall well-being and promote long-term health.

🍊 KEY NUTRIENTS IN FRUITS

🍌 Banana Calories: ~105 (per medium banana) Key Nutrients: Potassium, Vitamin B6, Vitamin C, Fiber Health Benefits: Supports heart health, aids digestion, and provides a quick energy boost. 🍎 Apple Calories: ~95 (per medium apple) Key Nutrients: Fiber, Vitamin C, Potassium, Antioxidants Health Benefits: Promotes gut health, may reduce risk of chronic disease, supports weight management. 🍓 Strawberry Calories: ~50 (per cup, sliced) Key Nutrients: Vitamin C, Manganese, Folate, Fiber Health Benefits: Rich in antioxidants, supports immune function, anti-inflammatory properties. 🍊 Orange Calories: ~62 (per medium orange) Key Nutrients: Vitamin C, Fiber, Folate, Potassium Health Benefits: Boosts immune system, supports skin health, aids iron absorption. 🍇 Grapes Calories: ~104 (per cup) Key Nutrients: Vitamin K, Vitamin C, Antioxidants (resveratrol) Health Benefits: Heart-healthy, may improve brain function, anti-aging benefits. 🥭 Mango Calories: ~99 (per cup, sliced) Key Nutrients: Vitamin C, Vitamin A, Folate, Fiber Health Benefits: Good for skin and eyes, aids digestion, supports immune health.

Tips for Adding More Fruit to Your Diet

  • Blend into smoothies 🍓🍌🥭
  • Add to salads or yogurt 🥗
  • Use as a natural sweetener in oatmeal or baking 🍰
  • Freeze for a refreshing summer snack ❄️🍇
